# DBA_SCHEDULER_WINGROUP_MEMBERS

DBA_SCHEDULER_WINGROUP_MEMBERS displays the members of all Scheduler window groups in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_SCHEDULER_WINGROUP_MEMBERS .
