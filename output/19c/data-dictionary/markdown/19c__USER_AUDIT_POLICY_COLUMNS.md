# USER_AUDIT_POLICY_COLUMNS

USER_AUDIT_POLICY_COLUMNS describes the fine-grained auditing policy columns on the tables and views owned by the current user. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_AUDIT_POLICY_COLUMNS .
