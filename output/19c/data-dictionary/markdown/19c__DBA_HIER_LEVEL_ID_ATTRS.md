# DBA_HIER_LEVEL_ID_ATTRS

DBA_HIER_LEVEL_ID_ATTRS describes the attributes that uniquely identify members of the levels of all hierarchies in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_HIER_LEVEL_ID_ATTRS .
