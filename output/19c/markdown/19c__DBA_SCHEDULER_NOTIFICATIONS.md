# DBA_SCHEDULER_NOTIFICATIONS

DBA_SCHEDULER_NOTIFICATIONS displays information about the E-mail notifications for all jobs in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_SCHEDULER_NOTIFICATIONS .
