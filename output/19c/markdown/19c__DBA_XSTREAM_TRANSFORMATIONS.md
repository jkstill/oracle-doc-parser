# DBA_XSTREAM_TRANSFORMATIONS

DBA_XSTREAM_TRANSFORMATIONS displays information about all XStream transformations available on a system, in order of execution. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_XSTREAM_TRANSFORMATIONS .
