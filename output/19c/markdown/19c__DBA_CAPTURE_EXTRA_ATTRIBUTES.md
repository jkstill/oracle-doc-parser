# DBA_CAPTURE_EXTRA_ATTRIBUTES

DBA_CAPTURE_EXTRA_ATTRIBUTES displays information about the extra attributes for all capture processes in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_CAPTURE_EXTRA_ATTRIBUTES .
