# DBA_ANALYTIC_VIEW_MEAS_CLS_AE

DBA_ANALYTIC_VIEW_MEAS_CLS_AE describes the classifications of the measures of all analytic views (across all editions) in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_ANALYTIC_VIEW_MEAS_CLS_AE .
