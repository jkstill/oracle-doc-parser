# DBA_XSTREAM_ADMINISTRATOR

DBA_XSTREAM_ADMINISTRATOR displays information about the users who have been granted privileges to be XStream administrators by procedures in the DBMS_XSTREAM_AUTH package. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_XSTREAM_ADMINISTRATOR .
