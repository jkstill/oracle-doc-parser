# DBA_CUBE_ATTR_VISIBILITY

DBA_CUBE_ATTR_VISIBILITY describes all OLAP attributes visible for the dimensions, hierarchies, and levels in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_CUBE_ATTR_VISIBILITY .
