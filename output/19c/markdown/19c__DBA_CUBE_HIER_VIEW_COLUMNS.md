# DBA_CUBE_HIER_VIEW_COLUMNS

DBA_CUBE_HIER_VIEW_COLUMNS describes the columns of the relational hierarchy views of all OLAP cube dimensions in the database. Its columns are the same as those in ALL_CUBE_HIER_VIEW_COLUMNS .
