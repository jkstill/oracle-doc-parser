# USER_EXTENTS

USER_EXTENTS describes the extents comprising the segments owned by the current user's objects. Its columns (except for OWNER , FILE_ID , BLOCK_ID , and RELATIVE_FNO ) are the same as those in DBA_EXTENTS .
