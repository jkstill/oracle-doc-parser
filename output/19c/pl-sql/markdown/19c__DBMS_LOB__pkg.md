# DBMS_LOB

The DBMS_LOB package provides subprograms to operate on BLOBs , CLOBs , NCLOBs , BFILEs , and temporary LOBs . You can use DBMS_LOB to access and manipulate specific parts of a LOB or complete LOBs.
